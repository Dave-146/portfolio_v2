North Ventures Branding

Designed North Ventures' brand and app to inspire adventure and let users customize their Canadian tours.

Brand Design Mobile App Visual Identity 2024

Project Overview

North Ventures is a Canadian adventure tourism company that needed a complete brand identity and mobile app design. The project focused on creating a cohesive visual system that captures the spirit of Canadian wilderness while providing an intuitive platform for tour customization.

Brand Identity

The brand identity was designed to reflect the rugged beauty of the Canadian wilderness while maintaining a modern, approachable feel that appeals to adventure seekers.

Logo Design

The logo combines geometric elements representing mountain peaks with organic curves suggesting flowing rivers, creating a balance between structure and nature.

Color Palette

A carefully selected color palette inspired by Canadian landscapes, featuring deep forest greens, warm earth tones, and crisp whites.

Typography

Typography choices emphasize readability and personality, with a combination of strong sans-serif fonts for headings and clean, legible fonts for body text.

Design Process

Research & Discovery

Conducted market research and competitor analysis to understand the adventure tourism landscape and identify opportunities for differentiation.

Visual Development

Explored various visual directions through mood boards, sketches, and iterations to find the perfect balance of adventure and accessibility.

Logo Development

The logo went through multiple iterations, from initial sketches to final polished versions, ensuring it works across all applications and sizes.

Mobile App Design

The mobile app extends the brand experience, allowing users to browse tours, customize their adventures, and book experiences seamlessly.

Results & Impact

The North Ventures brand identity successfully captures the essence of Canadian adventure while providing a solid foundation for the company's digital presence and marketing efforts.
