Sproot AR Wayfinding

A modern wayfinding solution for grocery stores, focusing on seamless shopping experience and customer engagement.

AR Technology Mobile App UX/UI Design 2024

Project Overview

Sproot is an innovative AR-powered grocery shopping app designed to revolutionize the way people navigate and shop in grocery stores. The app combines augmented reality technology with intuitive design to help users find items quickly, discover deals, and explore new products.

Design Process

Research & Discovery

Conducted extensive user research to understand grocery shopping pain points, including time constraints, navigation difficulties, and the desire for personalized recommendations.

AR Integration

Explored AR technology capabilities and designed intuitive interfaces that seamlessly blend digital information with the physical shopping environment.

Key Features

AR Navigation

Real-time AR overlays guide users to specific products with visual cues and directions

Smart Search

Advanced search functionality with filters for dietary restrictions, brands, and price ranges

Deal Discovery

Personalized deal recommendations and notifications for items on user's shopping list

Technical Implementation

AR Technology

  • ARKit/ARCore integration for device compatibility
  • Computer vision for product recognition
  • Real-time location tracking and mapping

User Experience

  • Intuitive gesture-based interactions
  • Accessibility features for diverse users
  • Offline functionality for poor connectivity

Results & Impact

The Sproot AR Wayfinding app successfully addresses key pain points in grocery shopping by reducing search time, improving navigation efficiency, and enhancing the overall shopping experience through innovative AR technology.
