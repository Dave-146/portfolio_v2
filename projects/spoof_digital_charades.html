Spoof - Digital Charades

Spoof blends digital charades with physical actions via a web app for hilarious, interactive fun.

Web App Game Design Interactive 2024

Project Overview

Spoof is an innovative web-based game that combines the classic fun of charades with modern digital technology. Players use their devices to display clues while acting out scenarios, creating a unique hybrid experience that bridges physical and digital entertainment.

Design Process

Concept Development

Explored how to merge traditional party games with digital interfaces, focusing on creating seamless interactions between physical actions and digital displays.

User Experience

Designed intuitive interfaces that support both individual players and group dynamics, ensuring the digital elements enhance rather than distract from the physical gameplay.

Key Features

Dynamic Clues

Real-time clue generation and display that adapts to different difficulty levels and player preferences

Multiplayer Support

Seamless group gameplay with synchronized displays and score tracking for competitive fun

Cross-Platform

Works across all devices, from smartphones to tablets, ensuring accessibility for all players

Technical Implementation

Frontend Development

  • Responsive web design for all screen sizes
  • Real-time synchronization between devices
  • Intuitive touch and gesture controls

Game Logic

  • Dynamic clue generation system
  • Score tracking and leaderboards
  • Timer and round management

Results & Impact

Spoof successfully created a new category of social gaming that bridges digital and physical entertainment, providing a unique experience for friends and families.
