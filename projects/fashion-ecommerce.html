The Challenge

The luxury fashion brand needed a modern e-commerce platform that could showcase their products while maintaining their premium brand image and improving conversion rates.

  • Outdated design and poor mobile experience
  • High cart abandonment rates
  • Limited product visualization options

The Solution

A modern e-commerce platform with enhanced product visualization, streamlined checkout process, and immersive brand storytelling.

  • 360° product views and AR try-on features
  • One-click checkout and saved payment options
  • Immersive brand storytelling and lookbooks

Process & Approach

Research

Conducted market research and user interviews to understand luxury fashion shopping behaviors and pain points.

  • User interviews with 30 luxury shoppers
  • Competitive analysis of leading fashion e-commerce sites

Design

Created a modern, luxurious design system that reflects the brand's identity while ensuring optimal user experience.

  • Luxury-focused UI design system
  • Enhanced product visualization components

Testing

Conducted extensive usability testing and A/B testing to optimize the shopping experience.

  • Usability testing with 20 participants
  • A/B testing of checkout flow variations

Key Features

360° Product Views

Interactive product visualization with 360° views and zoom capabilities.

AR Try-On

Augmented reality feature for virtual product try-on and visualization.

One-Click Checkout

Streamlined checkout process with saved payment options and shipping details.

Digital Lookbook

Interactive digital lookbook with shoppable product links and styling tips.
