The Challenge

The existing wellness app had low user engagement and retention rates. Users found it difficult to track their wellness goals and maintain consistent usage.

  • Complex navigation and overwhelming interface
  • Lack of personalization and goal tracking features
  • Poor visual hierarchy and accessibility issues

The Solution

A complete redesign focusing on user-centered design principles, simplified navigation, and enhanced personalization features.

  • Streamlined interface with clear visual hierarchy
  • Personalized wellness tracking and goal setting
  • Enhanced accessibility and inclusive design

Process & Approach

Research

Conducted user interviews and surveys to understand pain points and needs of wellness app users.

  • User interviews with 25 participants
  • Competitive analysis of top wellness apps

Design

Created wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes focusing on user flow and visual design.

  • Information architecture and user flows
  • UI design system and component library

Testing

Conducted usability testing and gathered feedback for iterative improvements.

  • Usability testing with 15 participants
  • A/B testing of key features

Key Features

Personalized Wellness Dashboard

Customizable dashboard showing key wellness metrics and progress towards goals.

Smart Goal Tracking

Intelligent goal setting and progress tracking with personalized recommendations.

Community Features

Social features to connect with friends and share wellness achievements.

Smart Notifications

Contextual reminders and motivational messages based on user behavior.
