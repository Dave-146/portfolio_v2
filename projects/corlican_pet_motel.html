Corlican Pet Motel

Redesigned a local business website to improve usability, navigation, and overall user experience.

Website Design UX/UI Design Local Business 2024

Project Overview

Corlican Pet Motel is a local pet boarding and grooming business that needed a complete website redesign to better serve their customers and showcase their services. The project focused on creating an intuitive, informative, and visually appealing website that would help pet owners easily find information and book services.

Design Process

Research & Analysis

Conducted user research to understand pet owners' needs when searching for pet services, including what information they look for and how they prefer to book appointments.

Design & Development

Created wireframes and high-fidelity mockups focusing on clear service presentation, easy navigation, and a warm, trustworthy aesthetic that appeals to pet owners.

Key Features

Online Booking

Streamlined booking system for pet boarding and grooming appointments

Service Information

Clear presentation of all services with pricing and detailed descriptions

Photo Gallery

Showcase of the facility and happy pets to build trust and confidence

Results & Learnings

The redesigned website successfully improved the user experience for Corlican Pet Motel's customers, making it easier to find information and book services online.

What Worked Well

  • Clear service categorization and pricing
  • Intuitive booking flow
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • Trust-building elements (photos, testimonials)

Areas for Improvement

  • Integration with social media platforms
  • Customer review system
  • Automated appointment reminders
  • Online payment processing
